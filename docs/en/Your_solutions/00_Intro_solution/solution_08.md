# Task 08 – Circular Motion

## Problem Statement

Centripetal acceleration on Earth.

## Theory

$$
a = \omega^2 R
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\omega = \frac{2\pi}{86400}
$$

$$
a \approx 0.034 \ \text{m/s}^2
$$

## Final Result

$$
a \approx 0.034 \ \text{m/s}^2
$$

## Interpretation

Very small compared to gravity.

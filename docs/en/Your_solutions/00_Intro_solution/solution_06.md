# Task 06 – Variable Velocity

## Problem Statement

Find position and acceleration.

## Theory

$$
a(t) = \frac{dv}{dt}, \quad x(t) = \int v(t) dt
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Acceleration:

$$
a(t) = 2t + 2
$$

Position:

$$
x(t) = \frac{t^3}{3} + t^2 - 5t + C
$$

Using $x(0)=4$:

$$
C=4
$$

At $t=3$:

$$
x(3) = 7
$$

$$
a(3) = 8
$$

## Final Result

$$
x(3)=7, \quad a(3)=8
$$

## Interpretation

Acceleration increases linearly.

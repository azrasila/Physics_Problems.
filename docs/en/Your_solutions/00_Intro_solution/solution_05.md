# Task 05 – Relative Velocity

## Problem Statement

Boat crossing river.

## Theory

To go north, cancel eastward drift.

## Step-by-Step Solution

Let angle $\theta$ west of north:

$$
5 \sin\theta = 2
$$

$$
\theta = \arcsin\left(\frac{2}{5}\right)
$$

Velocity north:

$$
v_y = 5\cos\theta
$$

Time:

$$
t = \frac{200}{5\cos\theta}
$$

## Final Result

$$
\theta \approx 23.6^\circ
$$

## Interpretation

Boat must aim upstream to cancel river drift.

# Task 03 – Path Intersection

## Problem Statement

Determine if paths intersect and when.

## Theory

Intersection occurs when:

$$
A(t_1) = B(t_2)
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Coordinates:

$$
2 + t_1 = 2t_2 - 1
$$

$$
8 - 3t_1 = 2t_2 + 2
$$

Solve system:

$$
t_1 = 2t_2 - 3
$$

Substitute:

$$
8 - 3(2t_2 - 3) = 2t_2 + 2
$$

$$
17 - 6t_2 = 2t_2 + 2
$$

$$
15 = 8t_2 \Rightarrow t_2 = \frac{15}{8}
$$

$$
t_1 = \frac{3}{4}
$$

Position:

$$
(2.75, 5.75)
$$

## Final Result

Paths intersect at:

$$
t_1 = \frac{3}{4}, \quad t_2 = \frac{15}{8}
$$

## Interpretation

They meet at the same position but at different parameter times.

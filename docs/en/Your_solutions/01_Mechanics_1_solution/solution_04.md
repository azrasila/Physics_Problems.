# Task 04 – Vector Calculus

## Problem Statement

Find velocity and acceleration.

## Theory

$$
\vec{v}(t) = \frac{d\vec{r}}{dt}, \quad \vec{a}(t) = \frac{d\vec{v}}{dt}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v}(t) = (6t)\hat{i} + (5 - 16t)\hat{j}
$$

Acceleration:

$$
\vec{a}(t) = 6\hat{i} - 16\hat{j}
$$

## Final Result

Velocity and acceleration above.

## Interpretation

Acceleration is constant → motion under constant force.

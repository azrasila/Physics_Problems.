# Task 07 – Parametric Motion

## Problem Statement

Eliminate $t$ and analyze motion.

## Theory

Parametric elimination.

## Step-by-Step Solution

From $x=2t^2$:

$$
t = \sqrt{\frac{x}{2}}
$$

Substitute:

$$
y = 3 \left(\frac{x}{2}\right)^{3/2}
$$

Velocity:

$$
\vec{v}(t) = (4t, 9t^2)
$$

Acceleration:

$$
\vec{a}(t) = (4, 18t)
$$

## Final Result

Nonlinear trajectory.

## Interpretation

Acceleration is not constant.

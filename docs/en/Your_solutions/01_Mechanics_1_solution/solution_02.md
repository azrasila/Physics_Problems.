# Task 02 – Range Optimization

## Problem Statement

Show that maximum range occurs at $\theta = 45^\circ$.

## Theory

Range formula:

$$
R(\theta) = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Maximize $\sin(2\theta)$.

Maximum of sine:

$$
\sin(2\theta) = 1
$$

$$
2\theta = 90^\circ \Rightarrow \theta = 45^\circ
$$

## Final Result

$$
\theta_{\text{max}} = 45^\circ
$$

## Interpretation

The optimal angle balances horizontal and vertical motion equally.

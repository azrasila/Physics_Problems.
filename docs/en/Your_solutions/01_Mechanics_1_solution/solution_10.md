# Task 10 – Kinematics

## Problem Statement

Analyze 3D motion.

## Theory

Parametric equations describe a helix.

## Step-by-Step Solution

Projection:

$$
\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1
$$

Velocity magnitude:

$$
|\vec{v}| = \sqrt{a^2 \omega^2 \sin^2(\omega t) + b^2 \omega^2 \cos^2(\omega t) + b^2}
$$

Path length:

$$
s = \int_0^{t_0} |\vec{v}| dt
$$

## Final Result

Helical motion.

## Interpretation

Combination of circular and linear motion.

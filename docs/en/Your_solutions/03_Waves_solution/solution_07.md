# 🌊 Wave Equation Parameters

## 📌 Problem

![Wave](images/wave.png)

Given:

$$
y(x,t) = 0.05 \sin(2\pi x - 50\pi t)
$$

---

## 📘 Theory

$$
y(x,t) = A \sin(kx - \omega t)
$$

* $A$ → amplitude
* $k$ → wave number
* $\omega$ → angular frequency

---

## 🔑 Key Relations

$$
k = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda}
$$

$$
\omega = 2\pi f
$$

$$
v = \frac{\omega}{k}
$$

---

## 🧩 Solution

### Step 1

* $A = 0.05$
* $k = 2\pi$
* $\omega = 50\pi$

---

### Step 2

$$
\lambda = 1 \ \text{m}
$$

---

### Step 3

$$
f = 25 \ \text{Hz}
$$

---

### Step 4

$$
v = 25 \ \text{m/s}
$$

---

## ✅ Final Results

* Amplitude: $0.05$
* Wavelength: $1 \text{ m}$
* Frequency: $25 \text{ Hz}$
* Wave Speed: $25 \text{ m/s}$

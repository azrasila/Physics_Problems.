# Problem 8 — Lorentz Force

## 📘 Theory
Lorentz force:

F = qvB sinθ  

If perpendicular:

F = qvB

---

## 🧮 Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Identify given values
Charge q  
Velocity v  
Magnetic field B  

---

### Step 2: Check angle
Given perpendicular motion:

θ = 90° → sinθ = 1  

---

### Step 3: Apply formula

F = qvB  

---

## ✅ Final Answer
F = qvB

---

## 💡 Key Insight
Magnetic force depends on **motion + angle**

# Problem 10 — Force on a Current-Carrying Wire

## 📘 Theory
Magnetic force on a wire:

F = BIL sinθ  

---

## 🧮 Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Given

B = 0.5 T  
I = 10 A  
L = 2 m  

---

### Step 2: Substitute

F = 0.5 × 10 × 2 × sinθ  
F = 10 sinθ  

---

### Step 3: Evaluate cases

Case (a): θ = 0°  
sin0 = 0 → F = 0  

---

Case (b): θ = 90°  
sin90 = 1 → F = 10 N  

---

Case (c): θ = 30°  
sin30 = 0.5 → F = 5 N  

---

## ✅ Final Answer
F = BIL sinθ

---

## 💡 Key Insight
Force depends on **angle between wire and field**

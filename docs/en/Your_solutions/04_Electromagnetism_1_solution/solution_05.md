# Problem 5 — Electric Field for Levitation

## 📘 Theory
For levitation:

Electric force = gravitational force  

qE = mg

---

## 🧮 Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Understand the condition
To levitate:
- Upward electric force must balance weight

---

### Step 2: Write equation

qE = mg

---

### Step 3: Solve for electric field

E = mg / q

---

### Step 4: Substitute values

m = 1.67×10⁻²⁷ kg  
g = 9.8 m/s²  
q = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹ C  

---

### Step 5: Compute

E ≈ (1.67×10⁻²⁷ × 9.8) / (1.6×10⁻¹⁹)  
≈ 1.0×10⁻⁷ N/C  

---

## ✅ Final Answer
E ≈ 1.0×10⁻⁷ N/C

---

## 💡 Key Insight
Even a **tiny electric field** can balance gravity on subatomic particles.

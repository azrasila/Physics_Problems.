# Problem 7 — Cyclotron Motion

## 📘 Theory
Magnetic force provides centripetal force:

qvB = mv² / r  

So:

r = mv / (qB)

---

## 🧮 Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Energy gain

Electron accelerated by voltage:

qV = ½mv²  

---

### Step 2: Solve for velocity

v = √(2qV / m)

---

### Step 3: Substitute into radius formula

r = (m / qB) × √(2qV / m)

---

### Step 4: Insert values

q = 1.6×10⁻¹⁹  
m = 9.11×10⁻³¹  
V = 5000  
B = 0.1  

---

### Step 5: Compute

r ≈ 7.5×10⁻³ m  

---

## ✅ Final Answer
r ≈ 7.5 mm

---

## 💡 Key Insight
Magnetic field bends motion into a **circle**, not changing speed.

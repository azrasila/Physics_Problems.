# Problem 9 — Vector Lorentz Force

## 📘 Theory
Vector form:

F = q (v × B)

Magnitude:

F = qvB sinθ  

---

## 🧮 Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Understand cross product
Force is perpendicular to both:
- velocity
- magnetic field  

---

### Step 2: Determine angle

θ = angle between v and B  

---

### Step 3: Apply magnitude formula

F = qvB sinθ  

---

### Step 4: Interpret

- θ = 90° → max force  
- θ = 0° → zero force  

---

## ✅ Final Answer
F = qvB sinθ

---

## 💡 Key Insight
Direction is given by **right-hand rule**
